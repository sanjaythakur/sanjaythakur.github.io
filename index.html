Hi, I'm Sanjay. A perpetual Markov state, I take life one state at a time, always moving forward, guided by purpose. My deepest drive lies in building things that expand human understanding, nurture free critical thinking, and unlock our collective potential.

Over the next few years, my mission is clear: to build technologies that address India's most pressing challenges with the power to scale globally. There is no bigger privilege than working on something that you are an expert in and that solves a real problem.

Why India? Because we're at a pivotal juncture. On one hand, we are facing deep, complex issues that wear us down, yet on the other hand, we are surrounded by incredible stories of resilience and possibility. This is our moment to rise, with innovation, urgency, and bold ideas. So, stay tuned, there's a lot coming.
