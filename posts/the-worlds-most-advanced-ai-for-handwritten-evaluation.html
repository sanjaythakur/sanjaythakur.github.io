Why This Was "Impossible" Until Now

Most AI companies gave up because handwritten evaluation requires solving multiple impossibilities simultaneously. It's a technical Everest that demands mastery across three critical layers:

Technical Challenges That Defeated Others

  • The Recognition Layer: Deciphering handwriting across 50+ languages and scripts, processing poor-quality phone photos, handling writing that spans margins and overlaps diagrams
  • The Understanding Layer: Interpreting context-dependent diagrams, connecting scattered text with visual elements, maintaining narrative coherence across multi-page responses
  • The Expertise Layer: Applying subject-matter knowledge across medical, legal, engineering, and history domains while evaluating creativity and insight
