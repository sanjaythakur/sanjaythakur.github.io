About Me

Get to know the person behind the blog.

Hi again! I'm Sanjay, a technologist passionate about making education accessible globally through AI. After my journey with Markovian AI (acquired in 2023) and leading engineering at Curium in Singapore, I'm now fully focused on Widushi.

My mission at Widushi is reimagining how students learn through personalized AI tutoring. Our mission is to democratize high-quality education, making personalized learning accessible to students regardless of geographic or economic barriers.
