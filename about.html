About Me

Get to know the person behind the blog.

Hi again! I'm Sanjay. Previously, I founded Markovian AI which was acquired in 2023. Between 2023 and June 2025, I led engineering at Curium (Singapore), building real-time, high-throughput IoT systems for asset tracking.

With a CS Master's from Mila/McGill and undergrad from MNIT Jaipur, I'm driven by solving meaningful problems with technology. This mission fuels me daily. Stay tuned — the best is yet to come.

Interesting Things I've Done So Far

  • I built and sold Markovian AI which was a data-search engine in Toronto. Think of it as a Google-for-enterprises before things like RAG, LLM was a cool thing.
  • I gained the dark knowledge of controlling ML behavior at the best ML research institute in the world – Mila and published at top-tier conferences. See my Google Scholar page here. To date I have accumulated over 700 citations.
  • At the age of 19 years, I built and patented an app that was purchased by the Indian Railways. I owe it to Zine which facilitated this opportunity when I was pursuing my Bachelor's degree.
  • During my third year of undergraduate, I built a Smartmeter for home automation, power saving and power grid balance. It was patented and awarded the runner-up prize at GridTech'15 International Conference & Exhibition.
  • I have been part of the only team that has won the most popular sports tournament of my university called Hong Kong Sixes twice in the 80+ years of university history.
